# Flying Door Activation

When fly mode is enabled, `Up` is a movement direction, not just an interaction button.

To avoid accidental room transitions while flying upward, door-style loading zones require a deliberate double-tap-up gesture while fly mode is active.

- Not flying: press `Up` to enter a door.
- Flying: double-tap `Up` to enter a door.
- Edge exits: still trigger automatically by moving through wall openings.

This keeps flying expressive while preserving quick room traversal.
